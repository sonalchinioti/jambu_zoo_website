WELCOME TO THE NEW LANDMARK OF J&K

Conserving Wild heritage OF THE PLANET.

Created with a vision to spread awareness about the conversation of wildlife and to create an environment for birds and animals similar to the natural habitat, Jambu Zoo is the new landmark on the map of Jammu and Kashmir. Perfectly located on NH-44, on the way to The holy shrine of Shri Mata Vaishno Devi, Jambu Zoo is a unique setting for visitors to experience nature and wildlife.Via Jambu Zoo the Department of Wildlife Protection of the UT of Jammu and Kashmir is determined to educate and sensitize the visitors, especially the young generation about the importance of nature conservation and sustainable coexistence of humans and wildlife on the planet
